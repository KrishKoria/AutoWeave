## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed my execution limit?

For Plus and Pro tiers, executions will be paused when you reach your limit. You can upgrade to continue, or wait until your next billing cycle. Enterprise customers have unlimited executions.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes! All new users get a 14-day free trial of AutoWeave Pro to explore all features before committing.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and for Enterprise customers, we offer invoicing and custom payment terms.

### Can I get a discount for annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing saves you 17% compared to monthly billing for both Plus and Pro tiers.
